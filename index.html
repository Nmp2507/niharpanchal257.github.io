About Me

Licensed Professional Engineer committed to advancing sustainable energy infrastructure

As a Licensed Professional Engineer (P.E.) with over five years of specialized experience in power systems engineering, I bring comprehensive expertise in protection and coordination studies, relay settings development, and substation design for utility-scale renewable energy projects. My career has been dedicated to ensuring the safety, reliability, and efficiency of electrical power systems across wind and solar installations throughout the United States.

I hold a Master of Engineering in Electrical Engineering from the University of Idaho and a Bachelor of Technology in Electrical Engineering from Indus University, India. My technical foundation is built on rigorous coursework in power system protection, relaying, power quality, and utility applications of power electronics.

Throughout my professional journey, I have successfully delivered protection schemes for numerous 115kV, 230kV, and 345kV substations, ensuring compliance with IEEE, NERC, and NFPA standards. My expertise extends to arc flash analysis, harmonic studies, short circuit analysis, and the implementation of sophisticated protection logics using industry-leading relay systems including SEL and GE platforms.
